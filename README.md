
## Moon Rock Enterprise

### What It's About:
- The website is created to allow driker's of this  enriching blend to connect and communicate worldwide.
- Allowing ease of communication of events, New Realeases & Taste-Testing opportunities!
- Notifications are sent once important Information is too be passed to our loyal customers either through the website itself or the users Email/Phone number which is required during sign in/ log in.

Notice: Email & Phone Number are only required for Log in & Sign In puporses only!

### How to Use It:
1. Open the website on your local browser.
2. Once fully loaded click on the Login Buttton on the Right-Center on the Navigation buttons of the webspace.
3. If already a user simply input your email/phone number and password.
4. If not a user, Click on the "sign in" button & input your email/phone number & New Password.
5. An Verification Code is to be sent on your email so as to provide proof of your ownership of the inputted email.
6. Once verified, you are an official Moon Rocker! You will receive a loyalty card which will guarantee a two year free entry to every Moon Rocking Event.
