
## RoadStar Motors

### What It's About:
- The website is created to allow riders connect and communicate nationwide.
- Allowing ease of communication of events &  new realeases of bikes
- Notifications are sent once important information is to be passed to the user either through the website itself or the users Email which is required during sign in/ log in.

Notice: Email & Phone Number are only required for Log in/Sign In & delivery puporses only!

### How to Use It:
1. Open the website on your local browser.
2. Once fully loaded click on the Login Buttton on the Right-Center on the Navigation buttons of the webspace.
3. If already a user simply input your email & password.
4. If not a user, Click on the "sign in" button & input your email & New Password.
5. An Verification Code is to be sent on your email so as to provide proof of your ownership of the inputted email.
6. Once verified, you are an official RoadStar!
